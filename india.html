India, officially the Republic of India, is a vibrant and diverse country in South Asia, known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and rapid development. It is the seventh-largest country by area and the second-most populous, with over 1.4 billion people. India is home to ancient civilizations, iconic landmarks like the Taj Mahal, and diverse traditions spanning languages, religions, and cuisines. Its geography ranges from the towering Himalayas in the north to lush forests, deserts, and extensive coastlines. New Delhi is the capital, while cities like Mumbai, Bengaluru, and Kolkata serve as economic and cultural centers. India's economy is one of the fastest-growing globally, making it a key player on the world stage.
