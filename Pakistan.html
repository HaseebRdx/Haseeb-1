Pakistan, officially the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, is a South Asian country known for its diverse culture, rich history, and stunning landscapes. Bordered by India, Afghanistan, Iran, and China, with a coastline along the Arabian Sea, it boasts a population exceeding 240 million. Pakistan is home to ancient civilizations like Mohenjo-Daro and Harappa, reflecting its historical significance. Its vibrant culture blends traditional music, cuisine, and festivals, while its geography includes towering peaks in the north, like K2, and vast deserts in the south. Islamabad is its modern capital, while Karachi, Lahore, and Peshawar are major cultural and economic hubs.
