The United States of America (USA) is a vast and diverse nation in North America, known for its cultural influence, economic power, and innovation. It is the third-largest country by both area and population, with over 330 million people. The USA is a global leader in technology, entertainment, and industry, housing iconic landmarks like the Statue of Liberty, Grand Canyon, and White House. Its 50 states offer a wide range of climates and landscapes, from bustling cities like New York and Los Angeles to natural wonders like Yellowstone and the Rockies. Founded on principles of democracy and freedom, it continues to play a pivotal role on the global stage.
